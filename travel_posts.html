Take a look at my travel posts, bringing their exceptional cultures right in front of your eyes.

Singapore, Singapore

Singapore is the home to a melting pot of cultures ranging from European, Southeast Asian, East Asian, and etc. This small island near the equator is the home to around more than 5 million people and considered one of the wealthiest city-states. It boasts of energy and innovation, striving to excel whatever they do best. But also, this small but dynamic island is somewhere I call home. Living here for 9 years, I have no regret.

Taipei, Taiwan

Taipei, the capital of Taiwan, is one of the most bustling cities in the world. Home to one of the most beautiful skyscrapers, landscapes, and vivacious night life, the city is the embodiment of culture. Upon my visit, I was amazed at the natural scenery and how it was well integrated into the lifestyle of Taiwanese livelihood. Taipei is a city I would love to revisit again.

Rome, Italy

Rome is something more than just the spot for a piping start of trends. It's a place that holds thousands of years of pure history possibly giving us explanations and answers we had about our past.

Upon my visit in the year 2019, I was amazed at how architecture from so many generations were able to stand still today. While standing in this small civilization, it was as if time had stopped and I was surrounded by the ambience of art.
